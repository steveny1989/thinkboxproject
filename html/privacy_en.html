Thank you for choosing ThinkBox! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information, and how you can manage it.

1. Account Registration and Login

1.1 Basic Information Collection
When registering or logging in, we may collect your phone number, gender, interests, and age to provide personalized services. Skipping certain information will not affect basic functionality.

1.2 Third-party Account Login
When logging in via a third-party account, we may collect your phone number for identity verification to comply with real-name requirements.

2. Product and Service Provision

2.1 Personalized Recommendations
To deliver our core features, we may collect personal data, browsing history, and actions (e.g., likes, bookmarks, shares). This helps us recommend relevant content. You can disable personalized recommendations in settings.

2.2 Permissions
For smoother functionality, upload and publishing features require storage permissions (photos, media, and files). Denying access will not affect other features.

3. Security and Risk Control

3.1 Device Information
To ensure account security, we may collect device information (e.g., model, network address, IP). Without this, risk verification may be affected.

4. Information Sharing

We will not share your data with third parties without consent, except for the following:

  • Providing requested services;
  • Fulfilling contractual obligations;
  • Protecting ThinkBox or user rights;
  • Complying with legal requirements.

5. Information Storage and Protection

5.1 Storage Duration
We retain personal data only for the duration needed to fulfill this policy’s purposes unless legally required otherwise.

5.2 Security Measures
Industry-standard security measures are in place to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

6. User Rights

You can view or modify your information by logging into your account. If you notice violations of laws or agreements, please contact us for resolution.

7. Account Deletion

If you no longer need ThinkBox services, you may request account deletion via **Me - Settings - Account and Security - Delete Account**. Deletion will permanently erase all associated data.

8. Third-party Services

Third-party services on ThinkBox may collect and use your data according to their policies. We recommend reviewing those policies carefully.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and service quality. You can manage or delete cookies via browser settings, but disabling them may affect some functions.
